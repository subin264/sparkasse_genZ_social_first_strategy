# Sparkasse Gen Z Social-First Strategy

**1st Place — GISMA × TLGG Consulting Hackathon (March 2026)**

## Overview
Competed solo in a consulting hackathon hosted by GISMA University 
of Applied Sciences and TLGG Consulting (Omnicom Group).

Developed an AI-integrated strategy to transform Sparkasse  
Germany's largest public savings bank into a Social-First 
financial platform targeting Gen Z customer acquisition.

## Hackathon Brief
- Client: Sparkasse
- Challenge: "From Traditional Trust to Social-First Relevance"
- Requirement: Integrate AI as a functional enabler (mandatory)

## My Approach
- Analysed Gen Z communication gap in German retail banking
- Designed platform native initiative beyond campaign level solutions
- Developed business case with ROI logic and long-term impact framework
- Integrated AI as a core functional tool throughout the strategy

## Result
Selected as the winning entry out of all competing teams.
